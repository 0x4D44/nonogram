## Ground-truth recap (severity-accurate)
- **A** blocker: `VARSSize≤600` clamp vs `Data[..,1..300]` → ~300 B past the 30 KB `New(PotentData)` block.
- **B** issue: Editer `K` unbounded — contained intra-object (IR-confirmed), corrupts adjacent clue rows.
- **C/D/E/F** array-bounds: backward-scan floor, `ResetData 20/25`, MakeText ceiling, guard-order.
- **H** issue: `CheckGroup` NIL-deref crash when status bar off + Recover.
- **I** issue: FollowRow 4th pass `XFilled` vs `YFilled` copy-paste.
- **J** issue: Yielder reentrancy (load/new/drop mid-solve).
- **K** issue: greedy mode non-interruptible. **L** opt: CalComplexity 16-bit overflow (display only).
- **M** blocker: HDROP truncated to 16-bit (runtime). **N** issue: DragQueryFile `iFile:Word` + WORD underflow.
- **P/Q/T** port: MDI menu state; **S** opt: HookProc WORD; **V** opt: version drift; licence-globals dead.
- **O** refuted-overread → cosmetic `%u` literal in Recover dialog. **R** refuted (no fix needed).
