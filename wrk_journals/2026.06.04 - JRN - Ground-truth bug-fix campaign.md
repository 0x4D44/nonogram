- **B** issue: Editer `K` unbounded — contained intra-object (IR-confirmed), corrupts adjacent clue rows.
- **C/D/E/F** array-bounds: backward-scan floor, `ResetData 20/25`, MakeText ceiling, guard-order.

- **K** issue: greedy mode non-interruptible. **L** opt: CalComplexity 16-bit overflow (display only).

- **O** refuted-overread → cosmetic `%u` literal in Recover dialog. **R** refuted (no fix needed).
